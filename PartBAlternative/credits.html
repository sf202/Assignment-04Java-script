Game Guidelines !

Objective:

The goal of the game is to match each of the 8 pictures with its identical copy.

Winning:

The game is won when all 8 pairs are successfully matched.

Ready/Start Button::

Click the "Ready" or "Start" button to begin the game.

How to Play

  • Click on a picture to reveal it.
  • Click on another picture to reveal its copy.
  • If the two revealed pictures match, they will stay open.
  • If they don't match, both pictures will disappear, and you can try again.
